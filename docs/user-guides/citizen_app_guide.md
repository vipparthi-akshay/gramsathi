# GramSathi — Citizen Mobile App User Guide

## Getting Started

### Download & Install
- **Android**: Google Play Store — search "GramSathi"
- **iOS**: Apple App Store — search "GramSathi"
- **Minimum Requirements**: Android 8.0+ / iOS 14+
- **Languages**: English, हिन्दी, मराठी, বাংলা, తెలుగు, தமிழ், ગુજરાતી, ಕನ್ನಡ, മലയാളം, ਪੰਜਾਬੀ, ଓଡ଼ିଆ, অসমীয়া

### First Time Login
1. Open the GramSathi app
2. Select your preferred language
3. Enter your mobile number
4. Enter the OTP received via SMS
5. Set up your profile (name, address, date of birth)
6. Upload your Aadhaar (optional but recommended)
7. You're ready to use GramSathi!

## Key Features

### 1. Find Government Schemes
- Tap **"Schemes"** on the home screen
- Browse by category: Agriculture, Education, Health, Housing, etc.
- Use the search bar to find specific schemes
- Tap any scheme to see full details, eligibility, and required documents
- Your personalized matches appear under **"Recommended for You"**

### 2. Check Your Eligibility
- Tap **"Check Eligibility"** on a scheme page
- The app automatically checks your profile against scheme criteria
- You'll see: ✅ Eligible, ⏳ Partially Eligible, or ❌ Not Eligible
- For rejected criteria, suggestions show how to become eligible

### 3. Apply for Schemes
- On a scheme page, tap **"Apply Now"**
- The app auto-fills your information from your profile and documents
- Review and confirm the application
- Track your application status in **"My Applications"**
- Notification updates at every stage: Submitted → Under Review → Approved/Rejected

### 4. Upload Documents
- Tap **"My Documents"** from the menu
- Tap **"+"** to upload a new document
- Supported types: Aadhaar, PAN, Voter ID, Income Certificate, Caste Certificate, Land Records, Bank Passbook
- Take a photo or select from gallery
- Documents are automatically verified through OCR
- Verified documents can auto-fill application forms

### 5. File a Grievance
- Tap **"Grievances"** from the menu
- Tap **"+"** to file a new grievance
- Select category and describe your issue (in your language)
- AI assistant helps draft your grievance
- Track resolution status in real-time

### 6. AI Assistant (Chat)
- Tap the **mic icon** or **chat icon** on the home screen
- Ask questions in your language:
  - _"PM Kisan Samman Nidhi ke liye kaise apply karein?"_
  - _"Meri application ka status kya hai?"_
  - _"Mujhe kis scheme ke liye patrata hai?"_
- Get instant answers with voice output
- 24/7 support in 12 Indian languages

### 7. Voice-based Navigation (IVR)
- Dial the GramSathi IVR number: `1800-XXX-XXXX`
- Follow voice prompts in your language
- Available operations:
  - Check scheme eligibility
  - Track application status
  - File grievance (voice recording)
  - Get scheme information

## Managing Your Profile

### Edit Profile
1. Tap the **profile icon** (top right)
2. Tap **"Edit Profile"**
3. Update personal details, address, or family members
4. Save changes

### Add Family Members
1. In Profile, tap **"Family Members"**
2. Tap **"Add Member"**
3. Enter their details (can upload documents for them too)
4. Their eligibility will be checked for schemes

### Notification Preferences
1. Go to **Settings > Notifications**
2. Toggle: Scheme Alerts, Application Updates, Grievance Updates
3. Preference: Push, SMS, WhatsApp

## Privacy & Security

- Your data is encrypted and stored securely
- OTP-based login for every new device
- Biometric lock available (fingerprint/face)
- Logout from all devices via Settings
- Data sharing only with your explicit consent
- Report security concerns: security@gramsathi.ai

## Troubleshooting

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| OTP not received | Wait 2 min, tap "Resend OTP", check spam folder |
| App is slow | Check internet connection, clear app cache |
| Document upload fails | Check file size (< 10 MB), format (JPG/PNG/PDF) |
| Can't find scheme | Use Hindi/regional names, check category filters |
| AI not understanding | Speak clearly, try simpler sentences |
| Grievance not progressing | Check expected SLA: 5 business days for resolution |
